Detailed Description

Damage calculation parameters for the mitigation pipeline.

Armor and resistance values reduce incoming damage. The formula: finalDamage = baseDamage * critMultiplier * (1 - mitigation) where mitigation = armor_or_resistance / (armor_or_resistance + constant).
